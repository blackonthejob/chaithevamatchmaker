Before You Swipe Right…

Things you're probably wondering.

Do I have to manage the VA myself after the match?
+
I don't just hand you a person and disappear. You'll get a clear onboarding strategy so you know exactly how to delegate, set expectations, and get your VA running without it feeling like another job on your list.
How fast will I get matched?
+
Most clients meet their VA within 72 hours of our discovery call. My match network is already vetted — I'm not starting from scratch, I'm finding your fit within what I've already built.
What if it's not the right fit?
+
Our discovery call is specifically designed to prevent that. I ask the questions most people skip — about your communication style, your pace, your non-negotiables. If something isn't working, I'm here to figure out why and fix it together.
How is this personal rather than a generic placement?
+
I spent years inside a high-growth brand managing real teams and real operations. I use that experience to assess fit — not just skill sets, but how a person communicates, how they handle pressure, and how they'll integrate into the way you specifically work.
What does a VA actually cost?
+
A skilled full-time VA typically runs $800–$1,500/month — significantly less than a US-based employee. My placement and strategy fee is always discussed transparently on our call. No surprises, no fine print. ♥
💡 Think of it this way: one month of VA support often costs less than a single hour of your time lost to admin work every day. The ROI tends to show up fast.
Who do you typically work with?
+
Mostly small business owners — e-commerce, coaches, consultants, service providers, and entrepreneurs doing way too much alone. If you're wearing every hat and feeling it, you're exactly who I'm here for. ♥
